# Tillo Hiring Context

This document defines the organisational environment in which hiring decisions are made at Tillo.

It helps interpret candidate signals relative to business model, delivery expectations, and operating scale.


---

# Company Overview

Tillo is the first global gift card network purpose-built for both brands and buyers.

We operate as a B2B fintech API platform connecting global brands and businesses through rewards, incentives, and gift card infrastructure.


---

# Vision

Turning rewards, incentives and gift cards into the currency of connection.


---

# Mission

Create value and connection through choice by empowering businesses worldwide with The Ultimate Choice™ for rewards, incentives and gift cards across global brands and open-loop prepaid products.

Tillo operates as:

- a B2B fintech platform
- an API-first integration partner

# Hiring Philosophy

Recruiting at Tillo is alignment work between:

- candidate capability
- team context
- business direction

rather than pipeline activity.

Evaluation principles:

Strong candidates progress.

Average candidates act as comparison profiles.

Weak candidates do not progress.

Signals should not be softened or inflated.

Calibration decisions should remain clear and evidence-based.


---

# Hiring Environment Expectations

Successful candidates typically demonstrate:

ownership of outcomes rather than task execution

comfort working in cross-functional delivery environments

ability to operate in partially defined problem spaces

evidence of improving systems rather than maintaining them

comfort working in scale-up execution environments rather than enterprise-only structures


---

# Role of Domain Context in Hiring

As a global fintech API platform:

domain familiarity is often important in:

- product roles
- commercial roles
- operations roles
- partnerships roles

and typically less critical in:

- engineering roles
- people roles

Role playbooks define where domain exposure is required or optional.
