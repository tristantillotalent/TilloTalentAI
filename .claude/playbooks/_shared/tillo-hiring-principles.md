# Tillo Hiring Principles

## Hiring supports long-term team effectiveness
We optimise for sustained delivery impact rather than short-term speed.

## Ownership matters more than tools
We prioritise evidence of decision-making responsibility over framework familiarity.

## Seniority reflects scope, not tenure
Years of experience are not used as a proxy for level.

## Acceptable gaps depend on team context
Missing skills are evaluated relative to delivery environment and support structure.

## Candidate evaluation balances signals
We consider:

- delivery ownership
- stakeholder complexity
- ambiguity handling
- execution consistency
- learning velocity

rather than single checklist matches.

## Domain relevance varies by role family
Transferability expectations differ depending on role context.

Domain experience is often critical in:

- product
- commercial
- operations
- marketing

and typically less critical in:

- engineering
- people functions

Role playbooks define where domain exposure is required.
